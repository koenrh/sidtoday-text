(U//FOUO) Mark your calendars and plan to participate in the First Annual Analysis Conference

Future."

(U//FOUO) The conference will give SIGINT analysts and other interested personnel

training needs and solutions, and measuring quality. NSA Director Lt Gen Hayden, SID Director
MG Quirk, and National Imagery and Mapping Agency (NIMA) Director Lt Gen Clapper (Retired)
are expected to attend.
